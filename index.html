Successful Stories:

Our first story comes from local painter Edwin Perez. While working nearly seven days a week to earn a living on the peninsula, Edwin received a micro loan from Create Opportunity for a total of $1,026 to purchase an electric paint sprayer. After each job he completed, he would make a payment, reimbursing the loan back faster than what was required. This not only made him more productive at his craft, but it significantly lessened the “wear and tear” he experienced on his body week in and week out. These relatively small loans can greatly transform the daily lives of our neighbors and local entrepreneurs. Help support local businesses and the local community by donating any amount below.

- Edwin Perez
